# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) Code of Conduct.

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the community.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at the contact address in `SECURITY.md`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
